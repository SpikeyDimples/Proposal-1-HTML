#LOCAL CHILLAS WEBSITE

This website is created for a local chill pub in the rural areas of breyten

The reason why I created the website is because i wanted to create a safe space for my communinty
accommodating even those who love fun,dancing,music,etc.

In the website i have a homepage which has the main picture of local chillas,
I have an about us page wich contains the information about local chillas and why it was created,
I have a contact us page which has the contact details for local chillas for those who are interested in working with us or 
booking ,I also have a menu page where the menu for local chillas is displayed because as much as local chillas is a chilling 
place I will not allow my customers to be hungry and last but not least I have a gallery page where people can see whats going on
at local chillas or see how the place looks and what is has before even stepping foot on the grounds of the area.
